Analogue and Digital Circuits


Module Overview

In this module, I learnt about the basics of analogue and digital circuits including:

  • Common electrical components, how they work, and their uses
  • Boolean theory and logical operations
  • Microcontrollers, sensors and actuators
There were 3 assignments for this module which all related to the client change set from eazysurve:

  • A circuit specification for a fluid handling system
  • A question and answer interview
  • A team project set by the cient (eazysurve)
