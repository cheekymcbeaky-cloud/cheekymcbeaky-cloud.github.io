Engineering Artifact - Water Monitoring System

For the engineering team artifact, I was tasked with being team comunications lead. as a team we where tarsked with creating a water blokagew detection system system. This project was a response to a professional brief from Network Rail to develop a robust, retrofit monitoring system for culverts. The client required a reliable and cost-effective method to track water levels and detect debris in hard-to-reach drainage areas to prevent critical infrastructure failure. The task demanded a resilient, low-maintenance solution capable of operating in harsh, confined environments to provide real-time data without the need for constant manual intervention.

The finished water monitoring system
